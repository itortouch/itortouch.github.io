Water consumption forecast

Company:

Aquaservice.

What:

The objective of the project was to make an accurate forecast for water bottle consumption consumption that will be made in each warehouse on a daily basis during the following week. The results gave accuracies up to 95%.

How:

The forecast is performed by combining different machine learning algorithms based on decision trees and gradient boost using stacking techniques. It takes into account operational variables, seasonality and even weather forecasts.

Message classification and priorization.

Where:

Aquaservice.

What:

When a customer sends a message to customer service through the APP, it first goes through a classifier that decides whether the message requires manual handling by an operator or whether it can be resolved through one of the automatic flows defined. If needs to handled by customer service, the message it's prioritized based on sentiment. Accuracies over 90% and flow sensitivities of over 80% are achieved.

How:

For the classification, NLP techniques based on the BERT Transformer are used, fine-tuned to the particular problem, considering only text as input. Using different pre-trained huggingface trasnformers.

My role:

Team lead and dev.

Meta-route optimization.

Where:

Aquaservice.

What:

With the acquisition of new customers, delivery routes become unbalanced over time. To correct this, routes are re-organized/optimized using specific software that performs the task. However, this generates advances and delays to customers by modifying the routes, causing significant losses of turnover (carry over) to the company.

How:

By reverse engineering the specific software, based on the results of the software, the input data is "tricked" by introducing priorities to minimize delays/previews.

My role:

Team lead and dev.

Where:

CPS Infraestructuras Movilidad y Medio Ambiente for Spanish public agency for traffic and transportation.

These displacements are concentrated when arriving by car to embark on the ferry of Algeciras generating queues that last for days with problems of water and food supply. The arrival of vehicles is anticipated several hours in advance to plan for peak hours.

Where:

Nextail.

What:
